Crepes Recipe

Description:

I’m not a great cook, so crepes are perfect for me. They’re simple, and I just fill them with whatever I happen to have. Sweet, savory, doesn’t matter—I just make it work.

Ingredients:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Butter, melted
  • Sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional toppings: Chocolate, fruits, whipped cream, syrup

Steps:

  1. Whisk flour, eggs, milk, water, melted butter, sugar, and salt until smooth; rest 20–30 min.
  2. Heat non-stick pan, lightly grease.
  3. Pour batter, tilt to spread thin.
  4. Cook 1–2 min, flip and cook 30–60 sec more.
  5. Repeat with remaining batter.
  6. Serve with desired toppings.
