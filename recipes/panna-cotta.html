Panna Cotta Recipe

Description:

Panna cotta is basically dessert magic in a cup. It’s creamy, smooth, just mix, pour, chill, and boom, dessert done. I like to throw some fruit or sauce on top if I’m feeling extra, but honestly, even plain it’s a little bite of heaven. Soft, sweet, and effortless, exactly how I like my desserts (and my life)

Ingredients:

  • Heavy cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp) or vanilla bean
  • Gelatin (1 tbsp powdered or 4 leaves)
  • Optional toppings: berries, caramel, chocolate sauce

Steps:

  1. Soak gelatin (leaves in cold water, powdered in a little water).
  2. Heat cream with sugar and vanilla (don’t boil).
  3. Stir in gelatin until fully dissolved.
  4. Pour into molds/cups.
  5. Chill at least 4 hours until set.
  6. Serve with toppings.
