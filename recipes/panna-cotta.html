Panna Cotta Recipe

Description:

An Italian creamy dessert made from sweetened cream set with gelatin. Smooth, silky, and melts in your mouth, often served with fruit or sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Heavy cream
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract (1 tsp) or vanilla bean
  • Gelatin (1 tbsp powdered or 4 leaves)
  • Optional toppings: berries, caramel, chocolate sauce

Steps:

  1. Soak gelatin (leaves in cold water, powdered in a little water).
  2. Heat cream with sugar and vanilla (donâ€™t boil).
  3. Stir in gelatin until fully dissolved.
  4. Pour into molds/cups.
  5. Chill at least 4 hours until set.
  6. Serve with toppings.
