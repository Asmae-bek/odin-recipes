Tiramisu Recipe

Description :

If you’ve never had Tiramisu, you’re seriously missing out. It’s creamy, coffee-soaked, and DELECIOUS. I like mine with a hot cup of coffee, served on a small plate, pretending I put in effort. The perfect treat to share… or to eat all by yourself when no one’s watching.

Ingredients :

  • Ladyfinger biscuits
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Strong coffee or espresso
  • Cocoa powder
  • Optional: a bit or ram, Marsala, or vanilla extract for flavor

Steps :

  1. Brew strong coffee or espresso and let it cool. Optionally, mix in a little rum or Marsala wine.
  2. Whisk egg yolks with sugar until thick and pale.Then add mascarpone cheese and gently mix until smooth. In a separate bowl, whip egg whites until stiff peaks form, then fold them carefully into the mascarpone mixture to keep it light and airy.
  3. Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the cooled coffee. Don’t soak too long, or they’ll get soggy.
  4. Place a layer of soaked ladyfingers at the bottom of your dish.Then spread a layer of the mascarpone cream on top. Repeat layers if desired, ending with the mascarpone cream on top.
  5. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (or overnight) so the flavors meld and the dessert sets.
  6. Just before serving, dust the top generously with cocoa powder.
